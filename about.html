Our Story

CTRL//FRAME began as a grassroots initiative to give young artists a platform to express themselves. What started as a small community project has grown into a vibrant organization with two creative branches.

🎬 Frame by Frame

Focused on motion arts—film, animation, and video storytelling. Youth learn to script, shoot, and edit their visions into reality.

🎨 Canvas CTRL

Dedicated to visual arts—illustration, design, and mixed media. A space to experiment, remix, and redefine the canvas.
